Welcome Message

From the Director's Desk

Welcome to the official web presence of the San Andreas Communications Department (SACD) — the central dispatch hub of ScenicRP. Our operators work tirelessly behind the scenes to ensure every unit in the field receives timely, accurate, and professional support, from routine patrol check-ins to the most demanding multi-agency critical incidents.


The SACD is more than a call center — it is the thread that holds every agency together. Whether you are a curious civilian, a prospective applicant, or an active department member looking for resources, this site is your gateway to everything SACD. Explore our divisions, meet our team, review our training standards, and find out how to join us.


— SACD Director

SACD Structure

Our Divisions

Three specialized divisions work in concert to uphold the highest standards of communications excellence.

Public Information Office
SACD · PIO

The Public Information Office is the voice of the SACD. Responsible for external communications, community outreach, and maintaining a transparent and professional public image for the department. PIO members serve as the bridge between dispatch operations and the wider ScenicRP community.

  • Head of Outreach & Relations
  • Outreach & Relations Coordinator
  • PIO Representative (×4)
Field Training Office
SACD · FTO

The Field Training Office is the backbone of SACD's workforce development. FTO personnel evaluate, mentor, and certify all incoming dispatchers, ensuring every operator meets the department's rigorous standards before going live on the console.

  • FTO Director
  • FTO Assistant Director
  • FTO Manager
  • FTO Supervisor
  • FTO Lead
Internal Affairs Division
SACD · IA

The Internal Affairs Division ensures accountability and integrity within the SACD. IA investigators handle complaints, conduct internal reviews, and uphold the ethical standards that define the department. All submissions are handled with strict confidentiality.

  • Executive Director
  • Operations Director
  • Deputy Director
  • Supervisor
  • Advisor
